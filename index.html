Her Story

Michelle has spent most of her life working on Environment and Climate Change, Health, and Humanitarian (especially Children) campaigns in forms of visual designs (posters, flyers, banners, bookmarks, report cover, logo, icons, photography and photo editing), writings, and performing arts. Up until now, she has spent more than two years in acting directly on international campaigns and supporting Government of Indonesia’s programmes in the area of Environment and Climate Change, Humanitarian and Public Welfare, and Poverty Alleviation. During her study in attaining bachelor’s degree, she worked on several community development projects and the impacts were unexpectedly exhilarating. Working on community development, public services, and arts have always been her passion.
